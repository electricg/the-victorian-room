We Are...

At King William IV dead, his niece Victoria succeeded him in 1837.
She reigned for 64 years and became the symbol of a whole era, which was called " the Victorian Age".
She was one of the greatest regnant of Great Britain and her era was characterized by imperialism, changes but also moralism and rigidity. Her sons and daughters became related to the principal royal families of Europe.
